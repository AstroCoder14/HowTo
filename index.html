Pokemon

An example of what you will need:

Most people would think that you would need 20 Pokemon, 20 trainers, and 20 energy, but you actually need only 9-16 Pokemon. The following cards are from one of the best decks of 2023.

  • Mew V --- x4
  • Mew VMAX --- x3
  • Genesect V --- x4
  • Meloetta ---- x1
  • Oricorio --- x1

As you can see, there are 13 cards in this deck. It is okay to go over 16 cards, but that limits the amount of trainers and energies you can have.
